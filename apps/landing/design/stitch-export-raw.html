info Thesis Project

The Science of Survival in a Voxel World.

BERONG SMP evolved from an academic "Fire Extinguisher Prototype" into a comprehensive disaster simulation environment. Operating on Minecraft 1.21 via the NeoForge modding API, this platform provides a controlled setting to study human behavior and emergency response during simulated crises.

The environment rigorously tests participants' ability to navigate hazards, utilize safety equipment, and follow established protocols in high-stress, procedurally generated or meticulously reconstructed scenarios.
